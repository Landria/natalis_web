Kaamos

Empowering European digital rights with resilient, censorship-resistant P2P communication.

Why Kaamos?

In an era of growing digital surveillance and censorship, Kaamos offers a groundbreaking solution that enables secure and private communications. By leveraging advanced P2P architectures and open-source protocols, Kaamos allows individuals and organizations to maintain freedom of expression and control over their data.

Core Features

  • * Decentralized, censorship-resistant P2P network architecture
  • * Deployable on your own servers for full data sovereignty
  • * Strong privacy protections with GDPR-compliant design
  • * Adaptive routing and traffic obfuscation for resilience
  • * Open-source codebase fostering transparency and community trust
  • * Designed to empower civil society, researchers, and policymakers
  • * Integrates blockchain technology for decentralized identity and trust management, enhancing security and auditability

Deploy Kaamos Anywhere

Kaamos is designed to be deployed on-premises or in private cloud environments, giving you full control and flexibility. Whether you represent a research institution, civil society organization, or a technology provider, Kaamos can be tailored to meet your needs.

Seamless integration with existing infrastructure ensures long-term sustainability and innovation.
